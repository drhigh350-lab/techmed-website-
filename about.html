Built for Students Who Refuse to Be Average

TECHMED was born from a simple realization: most students are not limited by intelligence. They are limited by the absence of structure, strategy, and the right performance systems.

For years, students have been taught to work harder, read longer, and push through pressure — yet very few are taught how to think strategically, study effectively, and perform consistently at elite levels.

What started as a mission to help students prepare smarter for major academic examinations evolved into something bigger: a performance-driven ecosystem built for ambitious students who want more than average results.

At TechMed, we believe excellence is not accidental. It is built through clarity, discipline, systems, and intentional growth. That philosophy shapes everything we do — from academic training and mentorship to performance coaching, educational resources, accountability communities, and student development systems.

TechMed is more than a tutorial brand. It is a culture of structure, precision, growth, and elite performance. For the students determined to rise above confusion, inconsistency, and mediocrity, this is home.

What We Do

Strategic systems designed to replace confusion with clarity, guesswork with strategy, and random effort with structured performance.

01 Operation 100 Drills

Our flagship daily accountability system. Students solve 100 high-yield past questions daily for 60+ days. This intensive drill builds speed, accuracy, pattern recognition, and unbreakable exam confidence through deliberate, structured repetition.

02 UTME + Post-UTME Brainstorming Hub

A focused community for solving questions and tackling past questions. Students practice intensively, discuss tricky problems, and build speed and accuracy through consistent past question drills and group problem-solving sessions.

03 Resources, Guidance & Mentorship

Structured academic resources, personalized guidance, and mentorship designed to give students systems instead of scattered information. We help students build clarity, discipline, and strategic habits that last beyond any single exam.

04 JAMB Tutoring & Exam Intelligence

High-impact coaching focused on exam strategy, trap identification, time management, and thinking like the examiner. We don’t just teach content — we teach students how to approach JAMB intelligently and perform under pressure.

Meet the Founder

Wisdom Johnson — Tutor TechMed

Medicine & Surgery Student, University of Uyo

Wisdom Johnson, known as Tutor TechMed, is a medical student at the University of Uyo and the founder of TECHMED. He is not just a tutor — he is a strategic academic mentor who helps students win with clarity, systems, and intelligent preparation instead of confusion and wasted effort.

Before gaining admission, he lived the exact pain many Nigerian students face today. After secondary school in 2021, he wrote UTME in 2022 with high hopes of studying Medicine & Surgery. He was not admitted. He spent nearly three years at home — facing disappointment, pressure, and the painful reality that hard work alone is not enough when you don’t understand the system.

Instead of giving up, he studied the admission process deeply. On his second attempt, he scored 331 in UTME and 91 in Chemistry. He finally secured admission to study Medicine & Surgery in 2024. That journey taught him one powerful truth: most students don’t fail because they lack intelligence. They fail because they lack strategy, systems, and proper guidance.

The Transformation

  • From struggling with C5s to scoring 91% in UTME Chemistry
  • Total UTME score of 331 — admission to Medicine & Surgery at University of Uyo
  • Cleared 100-Level with a perfect 5.0 GPA using the same "Exam Intelligence" strategies
  • Created strategic e-books and materials that transformed thousands of students
  • Works as a UTME tutor with several top brands
  • Produced two generations of 300+ scorers — 2026 being the most massive
  • The highest JAMB scorer in Nigeria (372) and one of the Top 20 nationwide (366) came from his ecosystem
  • Built Operation 100 — where scholars solve 100 past questions daily, 60+ days to JAMB
  • Held masterclasses with 3,000+ attendees
  • Built TechMed — the strategic system students use to gain admission intelligently

Today, Tutor TechMed is not merely about one brand. It is about every student who receives value. Moments like 2026 remind us of something powerful: when preparation meets consistency, extraordinary things become possible.
